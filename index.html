We still believe that you should be able to keep yourself safe online. There are some simple steps you can do right now to protect yourself online.

  • Move your router away from your window so no one can see the passwords on the back of it.

  • Set your social media profiles to private so strangers can't see your posts.

  • Use complex passwords with a mixture of UPPERCASE and lowercase letters, Numbers and Symbols.
    Or, choose 3 random words - Example: CamelSundaysHamper159

  • Don't use the same password for every account.

  • Use multifactor authentication to add a second layer of security on your accounts. Most big services offer this now.

  • Your bank or other finacial institutions will never call or email you asking you to give them your personal details.
    If you are ever unsure if a call or email is legit. Hangup, do not respond and contact the company via their trusted communication platforms.
