We still believe that you should be able to keep yourself safe online. There are some simple steps you can take now.

  • Move your router away from your window so no one can see the passwords on the back of it.
  • Set your social media profiles to private so strangers can't see your posts.
  • Use complex passwors with a mixture of Uppercase, lowercase letters, Numbers and Symbols.
  • Don't use the same password for every account.
  • Use multifactor authentication to add a second layer of security on your accounts.
  • Your bank or HMRC will never call or email you asking you to give them your personal details.
  • If you are ever unsure if a call or email is legit. Hangup, do not respond and contact the company via their trusted communication platforms.
